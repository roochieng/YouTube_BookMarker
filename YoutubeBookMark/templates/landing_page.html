YoutTube Video Bookmarker

This is a website that enables you to bookmark YouTube Videos by copying and pasting the url to save it so you can come back later to watch, at your convenient.

Concentrate on what you are doing now and save that important video to watch later without loosing a sweat.

Forget about the headache of saving YouTube videos for watch later!

Features:

After creating an account and signing in here are some of the features you will access

  • Bookmarking a video: You save the video link, it stores video name, channel name and the date you bookmarked it
  • Accessing the bookmarked videos and watch them in YouTube by clicking the video name.
  • You can delete the bookmark at any time.
  • You cannot duplicate a bookmark. This means you can only have a single record of a specific video bookmark
  • If you deleted a video by mistake, you can bookmark it right back.
  • Home will list your lates 5 bookmarks, with the link to watch them in YouTube
