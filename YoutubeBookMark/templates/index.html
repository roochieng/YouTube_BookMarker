Overview

This is a website that enables you to bookmark YouTube Videos by copying and pasting the url to save it so you can come back later to watch, at your convenient.

After you have bookmarked a video can watch by navigating to home if it is among the last 5 bookmarks or through Dashboards to get all bookmars.

  • Home section shows the latest videos you have bookmarked
  • Dashboard section shows you a table of the bookmarks you have added.
  • Add Bookmark enanables you to bookmark a youtube video by copying the youtube video url, pasting in the tesxtbox and click Bookmark button.
  • You can also watch the video demo by clicking the Demo at the top navigation bar.

Note: We are not yet supporting playlists or non-YouTube videos.
